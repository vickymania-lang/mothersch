Our Vision

Our vision at Mothers' partner children school is to empower pupils to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school: respect, tolerance, integrity, discipline and excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote lifelong learning in an open and caring atmosphere that motivates pupils to be confident and responsible citizens in the society for a better tomorrow to themselves and the world at large.
